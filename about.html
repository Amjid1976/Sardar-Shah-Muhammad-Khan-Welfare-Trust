About Us

The Sardar Shah Muhammad Khan Welfare Organization is committed to serving the community through various initiatives aimed at improving lives and providing support where it is needed the most. Based in Rawalakot, Azad Kashmir, our organization operates on the principles of compassion, service, and dedication.

We run a religious institute and the internationally recognized Palm Tree Collegiate, which is dedicated to providing quality education to students in the region. In addition, we offer ambulance services to the residents of Village Namnoota, ensuring timely medical assistance in emergencies.

Our work also extends to supporting orphaned and needy individuals by providing them with access to education and opportunities for a brighter future. At the core of our mission is a commitment to fostering a sense of community and ensuring that no one is left behind.

Our Services

  • Education:
    Operating Palm Tree Collegiate to deliver world-class education.
  • Religious Guidance:
    Managing a religious institute to nurture spiritual growth.
  • Ambulance Services:
    Providing timely emergency medical services to Village Namnoota.
  • Support for Needy:
    Assisting orphaned and underprivileged individuals to create opportunities for a better future.

Our Vision

We envision a society where every individual has access to education, healthcare, and the resources necessary to lead a dignified and fulfilling life. Through our various initiatives, we strive to make this vision a reality, one step at a time.
