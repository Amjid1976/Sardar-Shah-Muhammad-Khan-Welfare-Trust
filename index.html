Chairman's Message

It is with great pride and humility that I address you as the Chairman of our Welfare Trust. Our mission has always been to uplift and empower those in need, providing them with the tools and opportunities to lead a dignified life. This journey would not have been possible without your unwavering support and generosity. Together, we have initiated numerous programs that focus on education, healthcare, and community development. These initiatives aim to create a lasting impact and pave the way for a brighter future for all. Yet, there is still much to accomplish. I invite you to continue this journey with us, sharing our vision and commitment to making a difference. Your contributions, whether big or small, bring hope and change to countless lives. Thank you for your trust and support. Sincerely,

Muahmmad Pervez Chairman Sardar Shah Muhammad Khan Welfare Trust

Our Vision

  1. To empower communities through education, compassion, and care, fostering a future where every individual thrives with dignity, knowledge, and opportunity.


  2. To build a society where women are spiritually empowered, equipped with religious knowledge, and capable of making meaningful contributions to their families and communities.

Objectives

  1. To provide quality education to children and adults, ensuring access to learning for the underprivileged and marginalized.

  2. To support orphans and needy individuals by offering shelter, sustenance, and opportunities for self-reliance

    .

  3. To nurture a compassionate and inclusive society rooted in ethical and religious values.

  4. We aim to provide accessible and high-quality religious education to women, enabling them to deepen their understanding of faith, practice their values confidently, and serve as role models in their communities.

Goals

  1. Establish and support educational institutions that provide free or affordable education for children and adults in Namnoota and beyond.

  2. Set up initiatives to support orphans and needy individuals with food, clothing, education, and healthcare.

  3. Develop vocational training programs to equip individuals with skills for sustainable livelihoods.

  4. Promote awareness of social and religious values, fostering community cohesion and mutual respect.

  5. Partner with organizations and individuals to expand the reach and impact of the trust's initiatives.
